# Wheel of Life - Client Intake Template

A coaching assessment tool used during Discovery sessions. Rate satisfaction 0-10 in each area. Part of the [[Coaching Business - Marketing and Onboarding|client onboarding]] process.

## How to Use

1. Have the client rate each section honestly
2. Look for the **gaps** - areas with the lowest scores
3. Look for **patterns** - what connects the high and low areas?
4. Use the results to inform coaching focus areas and goal setting
5. Revisit periodically to track progress

## Coaching Questions to Explore Results

- What would it take to move from a ___ to a ___?
- Which area, if improved, would have the biggest ripple effect?
- What does a "10" look like in each area?
- Where are you giving your power away?
