## Client Onboarding Process

Based on Jennifer Creed's (CPCC) "Power in You Life Coaching" template:

### Welcome Letter
Sets expectations for the coaching relationship:
- Client must be **committed to living an extraordinary life**
- Client must be **responsible and honest**
- Coach promises to love and support, give straight feedback, challenge beyond limitations

### Letter of Agreement
- Coaching approach: creative exercises, questions, listening, assignments
- **Confidentiality clause** included
- **3-month initial engagement**
- 2 sessions/month
- First session: 1-1.5 hours (Discovery + Designed Alliance)
- Subsequent sessions: 45-60 minutes

### Fees (2016 Template)
- $550 for 3 months (Discovery + 5 sessions)
- Potentially tax-deductible as professional development

### Client Questionnaire (13 Deep Questions)
Key questions to ask during Discovery:
1. Look forward 20 years - what speech would you want given about you?
2. What do you long to do with unlimited time and resources?
3. Who inspires you and why?
4. What's missing that would make life a perfect 10?
5. When do you give your power away?
6. How do you define success?
