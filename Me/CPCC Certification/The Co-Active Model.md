# The Co-Active Model

> "Co-Active Coaching is a powerful alliance designed to forward and enhance the lifelong process of human learning, effectiveness and fulfillment."

## The Model Structure

The Co-Active Model is a wheel/star diagram with three core principles at the center surrounded by contextual coaching skills.

### Three Core Principles (Center)
1. **[[Three Principles - Fulfillment Balance Process|Fulfillment]]** - Living a life aligned with purpose and values
2. **[[Three Principles - Fulfillment Balance Process|Balance]]** - Generating perspectives and making resonant choices
3. **[[Three Principles - Fulfillment Balance Process|Process]]** - Being present with what is, dancing with emotions

### Five Contextual Skills (Radiating Outward)
- **[[Three Levels of Listening|Listening]]** - Three levels from internal to global awareness
- **Curiosity** - Open-ended, provocative, non-judgmental questions
- **Intuition** - Direct knowing, trusting the gut, remaining unattached to accuracy
- **Self-Management** - Getting out of the way to hold the client's agenda
- **Forward/Deepen** - Moving to action while deepening learning

### Designed Alliance
The custom-designed coaching relationship sits at the foundation - revisited regularly to ensure alignment.

## Four Cornerstones (External Frame)
See [[The Four Cornerstones]] for full detail.

1. People are Naturally Creative, Resourceful and Whole
2. Dance in This Moment
3. Focus on the Whole Person
4. Evoke Transformation
