## QUICK REFERENCE — Augustine Diagnostic

| # | Beatitude | Stuck Pattern | Active Lie | What It Blocks |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Poor in Spirit | Self-sufficiency, performance, shame at need | "I must be enough before God can use me" | All subsequent stages |
| 2 | Mourning | Numbness / spiritual bypass OR grief as permanent identity | "Grief is weakness or faithlessness" | Meekness (ungrieved loss produces grasping, not surrender) |
| 3 | Meek | Control, contingency management OR passive resentment | "If I release control I will be unsafe" | Hunger (striving replaces longing) |
| 4 | Hunger | Shame-defeat, cycle of failure OR perfectionist striving | "I am defined by my failure. My desire may not be real." | Mercy (can't give from an empty account) |
| 5 | Merciful | Defense wall, withheld forgiveness OR compulsive giving | "Mercy makes me vulnerable. Guarding is self-respect." | Pure in Heart (divided motives; the ledger runs alongside everything) |
| 6 | Pure in Heart | Paralysis, motive-suspicion OR image-management | "I am fundamentally corrupt. Nothing I do is clean." | Peacemaking (distorted vision; own agenda enters the conflict) |
| 7 | Peacemaker | Conflict avoidance framed as grace OR truth without love | "Harmony is peace. If conflict exists, I failed." | Persecution (no capacity to hold ground under opposition) |
| 8 | Persecuted | Grandiosity (all opposition = attack) OR collapse OR bitterness | Varies: ego / results-dependency / unmerciful toward opponents | (Reveals which earlier Beatitude hasn't fully held) |

### Beatitude 8 Variants — Diagnostic

| What you observe | What it reveals | Where to return |
|---|---|---|
| All opposition framed as persecution | Ego still asserting — mixed motives unaddressed | Beatitude 3 (meek) and 6 (pure in heart) |
| Collapses when results don't come | Identity rested on confirmation, not foundation | Beatitude 1 (poor in spirit) |
| Holding position but growing bitter | Mercy not extended to those opposing | Beatitude 5 (merciful) |

### Intake Questions — Listen for These

| Question | What it reveals |
|---|---|
| "When things get hard, what's your first move?" | B1 — dependence vs. self-sufficiency |
| "How comfortable are you sitting with grief?" | B2 — permission to mourn |
| "Is there something you feel like you can't let go of?" | B3 — control / surrender |
| "Do you actually believe you can change in the areas that matter most?" | B4 — hunger vs. shame-defeat |
| "Is there a person you haven't forgiven — or feel like you can't?" | B5 — mercy |
| "How much do you trust your own motives?" | B6 — purity / self-distrust |
| "Are there important relationships with significant things unsaid?" | B7 — peacemaking vs. avoidance |
| "When you take a stand and face pushback, what happens internally?" | B8 — sequence integrity |
