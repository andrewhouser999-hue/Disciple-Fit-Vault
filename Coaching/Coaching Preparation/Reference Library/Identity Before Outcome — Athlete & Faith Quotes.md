# Identity Before Outcome — Athlete & Faith Quotes

> The through-line: **identity precedes behavior, which precedes outcome** — not the reverse. The athlete who waits to feel like a champion before acting like one never becomes one.

---

## "Be" Before You "Do"

- **Muhammad Ali** — *"I am the greatest. I said that even before I knew I was."*
- **Aristotle** — *"We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act, but a habit."*
- **C.S. Lewis** — *"Put on the new self... act as if you already are what you wish to become."* *(Mere Christianity paraphrase)*

---

## Sport Psychology / Performance Coaching

- **James Clear (Atomic Habits)** — *"Every action you take is a vote for the type of person you wish to become."*
  - Core model: **Identity → Process → Outcome** (not the reverse)
- **Amy Cuddy** — *"Don't fake it till you make it. Fake it till you become it."*
- **Maxwell Maltz (Psycho-Cybernetics)** — *"Your nervous system cannot tell the difference between an actual experience and one vividly imagined."*

---

## Athlete-Sourced

- **Kobe Bryant** — *"Be the hunter, not the hunted. Always attack."*
- **Michael Jordan** — *"I never looked at the consequences of missing a big shot. Why? Because when you think about the consequences, you always think of a negative result."*
- **LeBron James** — *"I'm going to be what I'm going to be."*

---

## Philosophical / Foundational

- **William James** — *"Act as if what you do makes a difference. It does."*
- **William James** — *"If you want a quality, act as if you already had it."*
- **Epictetus** — *"First say to yourself what you would be; then do what you have to do."*
- **Viktor Frankl** — *"Between stimulus and response there is a space. In that space is our power to choose our response."*

---

## Coach-Sourced

- **Bo Eason** *(NFL → performance coach)* — *"Who do you need to become to do what needs to be done?"*
- **Pete Carroll** — *"The only way you can become excellent is to believe you already are."*

---

## Faith-Based

- **Romans 4:17** — *"...calls things that are not as though they were."* — Abraham operating from declared identity before evidence.
- **Ephesians 4:24** — *"Put on the new self, created to be like God in true righteousness and holiness."*
- **2 Corinthians 5:17** — *"If anyone is in Christ, he is a new creation."* — Identity transformation as the theological ground for behavioral change.
- **Colossians 3:10** — *"...put on the new self, which is being renewed in knowledge in the image of its Creator."*
