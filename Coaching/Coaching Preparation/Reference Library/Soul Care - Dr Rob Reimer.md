You will never rise above your level of self awareness. The things we deny about ourselves are the very things that deny us from the fullness of God *(others)*. page 14

If we are going to experience true transformation, we must deal with the issues of the heart and not just simple external behaviors. (*mental assertions, the heart*) pg 19

As the medical system's core competency is sickness management, symptom treatment leaving the disease on its destructive course, so the church's core competency towards sin is sin management, leaving the underlying issue on its destructive course.

God is fare less concerned about our behavior than we think He is. He is much more concerned about our hearts than we'll ever know. pg20 *for out of the overflow of the mouth (and actions) is the issue of the heart, Matt 12:34, 35*

Three essential components that comprise an atmosphere where maximum life change can occur. It takes all 3 to create a culture of deep change.
1. Anointed Teaching-teacher has lived-into the teaching, anointed teaching carries greater weight. Freely you have received, freely give. Matt 10:8
2. True Confessional Honest, Open Community filled with Grace-Church leaders must create a grace-filled atmosphere where people can confess their sins.
3. Presence and Power of God-
