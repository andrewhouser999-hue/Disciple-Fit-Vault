## Top Schools for Cognitive Behavioral Coaching

### University Programs (Academic / Research Track)

| Institution | Program | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **University of Sydney** | Master of Coaching Psychology | One of the strongest CBC-specific graduate programs globally. Combines CBT, ACT, positive psychology, and coaching research. |
| **Henley Business School (UK)** | MSc Coaching & Behavioural Change | Top European program. Evidence-based, CBC-aligned. Strong research faculty. |
| **Oxford Brookes University (UK)** | MSc Coaching Psychology | Rigorous academic CBC track. Covers CBT, ACT, MBCT in coaching contexts. |
| **Birkbeck, University of London** | MSc Organizational Psychology (Coaching pathway) | Strong applied focus. CBC integrated into organizational coaching. |
| **City, University of London** | MSc Coaching Psychology | Research-oriented. Good ACT and CBC foundation. |

### Professional / Certification Track (Non-Degree, Practitioner-Focused)

| Institution | Program | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Beck Institute** | CBT Training for Coaches | Gold standard CBT training. Not coaching-specific but most rigorous CBT foundation available. Directly applicable. |
| **Association for Contextual Behavioral Science (ACBS)** | ACT Training (various levels) | The home of ACT. Practitioner training with supervision. Global network. acbs.org |
| **Centre for Coaching (UK)** — Stephen Palmer | Certificate/Diploma in CBC | Palmer is the primary academic architect of Cognitive Behavioral Coaching as a named discipline. Most directly CBC-titled program. |

| **Motivational Interviewing Network of Trainers (MINT)** | MINT-certified training | motivationalinterviewing.org. Required for competent MI delivery. |

## Pricing — What Free Cannot Get You

### Motivational Interviewing (MINT)

| Route                      | Price                  | Hours                    | What It Is                                                                                       | Competency?                                                                                                |
| -------------------------- | ---------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Self-guided online         | **$150**               | **12 CE hours**          | Recorded modules. Knowledge-level content.                                                       | Foundation only — no supervised practice included                                                          |
| 2-day in-person workshop   | **$575–$675**          | **~14–16 contact hours** | Live instruction from a MINT trainer                                                             | Small skill gains; skills deteriorate without continued practice                                           |
| Full competency (informal) | **$500–$1,000+ total** | **No fixed number**      | Initial training + practice sessions reviewed against MITI fidelity coding + ongoing supervision | No official certification exists — MINT explicitly states there is no "certified MI therapist or provider" |

**What $150 buys:** 12 CE credit-eligible hours — enough to understand the model, not enough to deliver it skillfully under pressure. CE credits are seat-time markers for professional license renewal; they do not equal competency.

### ACT — ACBS / Russ Harris (Psychwire)

| Route | Price | Hours | What It Is | Competency? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| ACBS membership | **$15/yr** | — | Access to resource library, community, online learning directory | No training — access only |
| ACT for Beginners (Psychwire) | **Visit psychwire.com/harris** | **16 CE/CME hours** over 6 weeks | Core ACT model, hexaflex, defusion, values tools. Forum access with Harris. | Strongest single entry for coaches — covers all six processes |
| Trauma-Focused ACT (Psychwire) | Separate course | **16 CE/CME hours** | Specialized population | Advanced — take after Beginners |
| ACBS peer review (trainers only) | **$80** | — | Voluntary review of training delivery — not clinical competency | Not a certification. ACBS has no official ACT certification by design. |

**What entry buys:** 16 CE-eligible hours covering the full ACT model. No certification issued or required — ACBS intentionally avoids a centralized certification system. Competency is self-assessed through practice.

---

### MBCT — Bangor University / Mindfulness Network

| Route | Price | Duration | What It Is | Competency? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Individual module (e.g., "Evolving in Mindfulness" 2026) | **£980 (~$1,240 USD) per module** | Single course | One component of the broader pathway | Partial — modules build toward the full certificate |
| Teacher Training Pathway (TTP) | **Est. £3,000–£6,000+ cumulative** | **3–4 years** | Progressive portfolio: training events + personal practice + supervised teaching + reflection + mentorship | Full Certificate of Competence in Teaching MBSR/MBCT — highest UK standard |
| MA in Teaching Mindfulness-Based Courses (Bangor) | Multi-year tuition | **4–5 years** | Full academic degree + certificate | Masters degree + Certificate of Competence — highest available qualification |

**What you're buying:** Unlike MI and ACT, MBCT has a defined, credentialed endpoint. It is a multi-year training program, not a course. Each module is one component of a pathway requiring personal mindfulness practice, supervised teaching hours, recorded session review, and portfolio assessment. No shortcut version exists.

## Honest Comparison

| Discipline | Entry Cost | Entry Hours | Certification Exists? | Time to Functional Competency |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **MI** | $150 | 12 CE hrs | No | 6–12 months with supervised practice |
| **ACT** | $15–$200 est. | 16 CE hrs (Beginners course) | No | 6–12 months with self-directed practice |
| **MBCT** | £980 per module | 3–4 year pathway | Yes — Certificate of Competence | 3–4 years minimum |

**For coaching practice:** MI and ACT entry costs are low and the absence of formal certification is not a liability in a coaching context — it is the industry norm. MBCT's certification pathway is designed for clinical teachers and is over-engineered for coaching application. Practical alternative: learn the MBCT framework and tools through the books and theory content; pursue the full teaching certificate only if you plan to facilitate structured 8-week MBCT programs.
