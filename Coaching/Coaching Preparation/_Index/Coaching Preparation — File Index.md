*Psychological and theoretical foundations. All entries are reference-level — understand the framework before applying it in session.*

- **Identity vs. Outcome-Based Habits.md** — Why identity-based behavior change outlasts outcome-focused; grounded in SDT, self-signaling, and contingent self-worth research.
- **Immunity to Change — Kegan & Lahey Framework.md** — Subject-object theory, immune system metaphor, four-column map, big assumption, and behavioral experiment for persistent change resistance.

- **Motivation Science — Full Framework.md** — Eight-layer motivation framework from biological foundations through pathology, with self-diagnosis tools and coaching questions.
- **Perfectionism — Adaptive vs. Maladaptive.md** — Maladaptive perfectionism as contingent self-worth (fear-driven, shame-based) vs. adaptive striving; requires behavioral experiments rather than cognitive work alone.
- **Positive Psychology & Character Strengths — PERMA Framework.md** — Seligman's PERMA model and VIA character strengths framework with Kingdom coaching alignment.
- **Reiss Motivation Profile — Theory & 16 Basic Desires.md** — Steven Reiss's 16 universal desires framework.
