## Referral Boundary — When to Refer Out

| Signal | Reason |
|---|---|
| Active trauma (especially childhood/complex) | Coaching re-activates without healing — requires trauma-informed therapy |
| Clinical depression, anxiety disorders, OCD, PTSD | Diagnosable conditions requiring licensed treatment |
| Suicidal ideation (any level) | Immediate safety protocol — always refer |
| Active addiction | Requires clinical/12-step recovery structure |
| Personality disorders | Deep structural patterns requiring long-term therapeutic work |
| Psychosis or dissociation | Outside all coaching scope |
| Severe or complicated grief | When grief impairs functioning beyond normal range |
