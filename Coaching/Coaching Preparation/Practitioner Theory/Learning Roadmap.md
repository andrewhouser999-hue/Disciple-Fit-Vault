## Why This Order

```
Tier 1 — What is the mind doing?
Tier 2 — Why is the person doing what they're doing?
Tier 3 — Who do they believe they are?
Tier 4 — What is their relationship to their own thinking?
Tier 5 — How do I work with all of this in a session?
Tier 6 — How do I help them rewrite the story and execute?
```

You cannot competently deliver Tier 5 and 6 without Tiers 1–4. Most coaching culture operates in Tier 5–6 without the foundation. That gap is where confident incompetence lives.
