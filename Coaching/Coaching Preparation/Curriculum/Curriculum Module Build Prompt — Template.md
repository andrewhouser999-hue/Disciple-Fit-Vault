# Curriculum Module Build Prompt — Template

*Reusable prompt template for building Disciple Fit coaching curriculum modules with Claude.*

---

## How to Use

Fill out every section before submitting. The more precise the input, the less rework on the output. The sections that most commonly get skipped — and cause the most retrofitting — are **Framework Integration** and **Deployment Trigger**. Don't skip them.

Sequence: submit the completed prompt → receive research → filter research → approve scope → build begins.

## Completed Example — "Keeping Eyes on Jesus"

*For reference. Shows what a filled-out prompt looks like.*

---

**TOPIC:**
Keeping eyes on Jesus — Matthew 14 / Peter walking on water. The mechanics of sustained attentional orientation toward Christ under pressure.

**PRESENTING PROBLEM IT ADDRESSES:**
Client is overwhelmed by circumstances — a wave has appeared, attention has shifted from Jesus to the storm, and they've lost interpretive authority over their situation. Deploys after a breakthrough moment in session when the client has been pulled up from under a wave and is in a posture of receptivity.

**CLIENT PROFILE:**
- Theological literacy: Moderate to high
- Church background: Evangelical / Non-denominational / Mixed
- Typical age range: 20s–50s
- Common presenting issues: Anxiety, identity confusion, spiritual discouragement, circumstance-driven faith
- Trauma prevalence: Moderate — present but not primary presenting issue
- Format: Individual sessions

**FRAMEWORK INTEGRATION:**
- Parable of the Soils: Each soil type maps to a specific cluster of attentional formation gaps. Rocky soil = training vs. trying gap. Thorny soil = turn-away-from gap. Hardened path = beholding depth gap. Module should make this explicit.
- Identity Installer / Lie Eliminator: The 7-part Identity Installer maps directly onto the Greek beholding mechanics (aphorao → putting off/on; katoptrizomenoi → transformation mechanism). Lie Eliminator runs the same counter-narrative structure at the lie level.
- 3-Part Session Prep: Lie Nukes connect to wave-narrative mechanic and counter-narrative gap. Eternal Foundation is the content of the received gaze (Gap 3). Wound Awareness explains soil condition.
- CBC: CBT restructuring = counter-narrative mechanic. GROW model = diagnostic + practice assignment. Biblical framework = Trinitarian agency and divine authority of the counter-narrative.

**DEPLOYMENT TRIGGER:**
After a breakthrough moment in session — client pulled up from under a wave, window is open, they're in a posture of receptivity. Not at intake. Not mid-crisis. The moment they're asking "why do I keep doing this?" or "what is this?"

**WHERE IN THE COACHING ENGAGEMENT:**
Middle sessions — after the eternal foundation has been established (Gap 3 minimally stable). Can deploy at any point a wave surfaces once foundation work is underway.

**DELIVERABLES:**
- [x] Teacher's Manual — comprehensive, includes all 4 parts; integrations with Soils, Identity Installer, Lie Eliminator, CBC, and 3-part prep model must be explicit
- [x] Client Study — 2-3 pages, rigorous, includes Greek; tone: rigorous-but-warm
- [x] Coach Diagnostic — 7 gaps, observable indicators + 3 diagnostic questions + recommended first practice per gap
- [x] Practice Reference Card — 13 practices, pre-filled explanations, client keeps it
- [x] Session Facilitation Guide — scripted language: handoff, Greek framing, return session protocol, practice assignment language

**THEOLOGICAL DISTINCTIVES TO MAINTAIN:**
- Agency distinction: client beholds (consent, effort), Spirit transforms (metamorphoumetha — passive). Do not collapse into technique or passivity.
- Covenant mutuality: the gaze is first received, then returned. Do not present as one-directional.
- Waves are a competing narrative (interpretive authority claim), not just external problems.
- Formation before crisis: this is a training framework, not a crisis management tool.

**TERMINOLOGY:**
- Use: wave-narrative, Lie Nuke, transitional instability zone, covenant gaze, return reflex, attentional formation
- Avoid: generic "quiet time" language, moralistic framing, secular mindfulness terminology without reframing

**DO NOT DUPLICATE:**
Soils framework document, Identity Installer document, Lie Eliminator document — cross-reference only.

**RESEARCH PARAMETERS:**
- Priority sources: Theological scholarship + practitioner models; distinguish well-established from emerging
- Flag contested claims
- Depth: Comprehensive across traditions (Protestant, Catholic, Orthodox contemplative) — flag tradition of origin for each source

**BUILD SEQUENCE:**
1. Research first — present findings before building
2. Andrew filters research and confirms scope
3. Build: Teacher's Manual → Client Study → Diagnostic → Reference Card → Facilitation Guide
4. Confirm each document before moving to the next
