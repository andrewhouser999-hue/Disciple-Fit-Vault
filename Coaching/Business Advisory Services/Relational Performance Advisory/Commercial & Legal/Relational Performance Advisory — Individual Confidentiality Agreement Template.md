# Relational Performance Advisory — Individual Confidentiality Agreement Template

> ⚠ **REQUIRES LEGAL REVIEW BEFORE USE.** This template covers the required operational elements of the individual confidentiality agreement. It is not a finalized legal document. Have an attorney review before presenting to any participant.

---

**Date:** ___________________________________

**Between:**
Andrew Houser, Relational Performance Advisory ("Andrew")

Role: [Title / Property]

---

## 1. Purpose

Leader is participating in a leadership assessment and development process as part of an engagement between Andrew Houser and [Organization Name] ("Sponsor"). This agreement describes what Andrew holds confidential, what the Sponsor receives, and what rights Leader retains over their own information.

---

## 2. What Andrew Holds Confidential

The following information is confidential to Leader and will not be shared with the Sponsor, other participants, or any third party without Leader's written consent:

- All content disclosed in narrative interview sessions
- Leader's individual multi-rater 360 data (both rater responses and Leader's self-rating)
- Leader's individual Behavioral Pattern Profile
- All content disclosed in individual coaching sessions
- Any personal history, health information, or other disclosures made in the course of assessment or coaching

---

## 3. What the Sponsor Receives

The Sponsor receives only aggregate, team-level findings — pattern distribution across the cohort, team-level activation data, and structural recommendations. No individual leader is identified in Sponsor deliverables.

**The Sponsor does not receive:**
- Leader's individual Behavioral Pattern Profile
- Any content from Leader's narrative interview or coaching sessions
- Leader's individual 360 data
- Any individually attributable information

---

## 4. Leader's Rights

- Leader retains ownership of their own Behavioral Pattern Profile.
- Leader may request a copy of their profile and any session notes summary at any time during or after the engagement.
- Leader may choose to share their profile with the Sponsor. This requires Leader's written consent to Andrew. Andrew will not share the profile with the Sponsor without that consent.
- Leader's participation in individual coaching is voluntary. Declining to participate in coaching does not affect Leader's standing with the Sponsor.

---

## 5. Limits of Confidentiality — Duty to Warn

Confidentiality under this agreement is not absolute. Andrew will breach confidentiality only in the following circumstances:

**Imminent risk of harm:** If Leader discloses intent to harm themselves or others, with a specific plan and means, Andrew will refer to emergency services and notify the Sponsor only to the extent necessary to protect safety. Andrew will not disclose session content beyond what safety requires.

**Legal requirement:** If a valid legal process (subpoena, court order) requires disclosure, Andrew will comply after consulting legal counsel. Andrew will notify Leader of such a requirement to the extent permitted by law.

All other disclosures — including non-emergency health disclosures, personal history, relationship difficulties, or mental health disclosures — remain confidential and will not be reported to the Sponsor.

---

## 6. Data Retention and Destruction

Andrew will retain assessment data, session notes, and profile documents for the duration of the engagement plus 12 months following the formal engagement close date. After that period, all documents will be destroyed.

Leader may request a copy of their own materials before the destruction date.

---

## 7. Acknowledgment

By signing below, Leader acknowledges that:
- They have read and understood this agreement
- They are participating in the assessment and coaching process voluntarily
- They understand what information is held confidential and what the Sponsor receives
- They understand the limits of confidentiality described in Section 5
