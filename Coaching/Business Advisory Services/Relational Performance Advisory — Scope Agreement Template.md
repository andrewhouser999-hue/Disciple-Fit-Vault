## 1. Engagement Description

**Service name:** Relational Performance Advisory

**Andrew Houser's role:** Advisory and Implementation Orchestration Consultant. Andrew designs the engagement, administers assessment instruments, conducts individual leader interviews and coaching, facilitates team sessions, and provides written recommendations. Andrew does not manage hotel operations, direct personnel decisions, or function as an employee or agent of the Sponsor.

**What this engagement produces:**
- Behavioral pattern profiles per participating leader (delivered privately to each leader)
- Team Aggregate Pattern Map (delivered to Sponsor)
- Structural recommendations report (delivered to Sponsor)
- Individual leadership coaching (delivered to participating leaders, confidential)
- Team facilitation sessions
- Month 12 checkpoint report (if engagement extends to Phase 6)

**What this engagement does not produce:**
- Psychological diagnosis or clinical assessment of any kind
- Therapy, counseling, or EAP services
- HR policy enforcement or personnel action recommendations
- Performance ratings or employment decisions
- Guaranteed business outcomes

> *"This engagement provides organizational consulting and behavioral development coaching. It does not constitute psychological diagnosis, clinical assessment, therapy, or EAP services. Andrew Houser is not a licensed mental health clinician. If any leader is identified as requiring clinical-level support, Andrew will recommend referral to a licensed clinician. The consulting engagement will continue with remaining participants."*

## 3. Confidentiality Structure

**Individual leader data:**
All content from individual narrative interviews, individual 360 data, and individual coaching sessions is confidential to each leader. This information will not be shared with the Sponsor or any third party without the individual leader's written consent.

**Sponsor deliverables:**
The Sponsor receives only aggregate findings — team-level pattern distribution, team-level activation data, structural recommendations, and Month 12 checkpoint data. No individual leader is identified in Sponsor deliverables without that leader's written consent.

**Individual confidentiality agreements:**
Each participating leader will sign an individual confidentiality agreement before assessment begins. That agreement describes what Andrew holds confidential and what the Sponsor is and is not entitled to receive.

**Duty-to-warn exception:**
Confidentiality is not absolute. Andrew will breach it only when:
1. A leader discloses imminent intent to harm themselves or others, with specific plan and means — in which case Andrew will refer to emergency services and notify the Sponsor only to the extent required for safety; or
2. A valid legal process (subpoena, court order) requires disclosure.

All other disclosures — including non-emergency clinical material — remain confidential and are not reported to the Sponsor.

## 4. Data Handling

- All assessment data, session notes, and profile documents are held by Andrew Houser and not shared beyond the terms of this agreement.
- Data is retained for the duration of the engagement plus 12 months following formal engagement close, then destroyed.
- No data is shared with third parties.
- Digital files are stored in password-protected systems not accessible to the Sponsor.
