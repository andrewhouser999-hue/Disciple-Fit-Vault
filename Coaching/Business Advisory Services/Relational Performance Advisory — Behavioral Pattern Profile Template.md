# Relational Performance Advisory — Behavioral Pattern Profile Template

**What this is:** Fill-in template for the Behavioral Pattern Profile — the primary interpretive deliverable of the assessment phase. One profile per leader. Andrew completes this after all three assessment methods are finished. The leader receives Sections 1–3. Section 4 is internal to Andrew only.

**Language standard (apply throughout):** Every behavioral claim must trace to a specific observation from the assessment data. No clinical labels. No trait attributions. No personality diagnoses. Every sentence passes this test: *"Could I point to a specific observable moment from the narrative interview, 360 data, or facilitated observation that supports this?"*

**Prompts in [brackets] guide what to write — remove them in the final document.**

**Sources for this profile:**
- Narrative Interview Diagnostic Checklist — this leader's completed copy
- 360 Scoring_Table tab — this leader's cluster means and divergence flags
- Facilitated Observation notes — this leader's section
