# Covenant Identity — Session Growth Notes Template
*Completed by the coach within 24 hours of each session. Target completion time: 5-10 minutes. This is not a session transcript or full clinical note — it is a growth record. Every field points toward the Growth Report. If nothing notable occurred in a field, write "none this session" rather than leaving it blank — absence of movement is also data.*

### Coach's One-Sentence Summary of This Session

*Write this first — before completing Sections 2–7. The summary written immediately after the session ends stays closer to what actually happened. Summaries written after full documentation drift toward narrative construction — toward what you assembled, not what occurred. Complete this field within five minutes of the session ending, then return to Sections 2–7.*

*INSTRUCTIONS FOR GROWTH REPORT PREPARATION:*
*When preparing the Mid-Engagement Snapshot (session 4-5) or Growth Report (end of engagement), read through all Session Growth Notes in order. Pull from:*
- *Section 4 (Language Evolution) — before/after language pairs*
- *Section 3 (Identity Votes) — specific milestone moments*
- *Section 2 (Growth Moments) — named growth evidence*
- *Section 6 (Spiritual Formation) — covenant identity layer movement*
- *Section 7 (Coach Observations) — externally observable evidence*
- *One-sentence summaries — the arc of the engagement told headline by headline*
