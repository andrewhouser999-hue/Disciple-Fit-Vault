# Covenant Identity — Mid-Engagement Snapshot Template
*Prepared by the coach for presentation in session 4-5. This is a client-facing document — it is read aloud together in session, not given to the client to read alone first. Built from the Intake Addendum (Part A and B) and Session Growth Notes (sessions 1-3 or 1-4). Target length: one to two pages. Tone: honoring, specific, and grounded in evidence — not motivational.*

---

## COACH PREPARATION NOTES
*(Remove this section before presenting to client)*

**Pull from these sources:**
- Intake Addendum Part A: A1 (presenting problem), A5 (God description), A8 (failure meaning), A9 (Q9 score), A10 (shame content), B2 (present-story statement)
- Session Growth Notes: Section 4 (language evolution), Section 3 (identity votes), Section 2 (growth moments), one-sentence summaries
- Re-administer Q9 in this session verbally before presenting the snapshot

**Presentation note:** Read the "Where You Started" column first — slowly, using the client's own words. Let it land. Then read the "Where You Are Now" column. Do not rush the comparison. The silence after is part of the work.

**What to watch for when presenting:** The client's body when they hear their own intake language. Surprise, grief, gratitude, recognition. Name what you see: "I notice something happening in you as you hear that. What is it?"
