# Covenant Identity — Arc Location Diagnostic
*Self-assessment for locating your current position on the covenant identity arc*

*Related: [[Covenant Identity — Diagnostic Lens Transition Logic]]; [[Covenant Identity — The Interior Life as Telos]]; [[Covenant Identity — Affections Discovery — In-Session Protocol]]; [[Covenant Identity — Stage 4b Implicit Level Practitioner Reference]]*

---

## How to Use This

This document locates you on the covenant identity arc at a given moment. It is not a one-time assessment — it is meant to be returned to as the arc progresses. The results are most accurate when you complete Part 1 while something is emotionally live, not in a calm, reflective state. The arc moves below the surface; the questions here are designed to surface what is actually operating, not what you would like to be true.

The arc runs:

**Stage 1** (Affections mapped) → **Stage 2** (False identity named with emotional contact) → **Stage 3** (Covenant truth engaged) → **Stage 4a** (Cognitive distortion blocking) or **Stage 4b** (Implicit memory barrier) → **Telos** (Interior life qualities becoming operative)

Most people are not located cleanly in a single stage. Part 1 finds the edge of your current work. Part 2 profiles where the telos is emerging or absent. Both together produce the arc location in Part 3.

---

## Part 1 — Stage Location

Complete each check in sequence. Stop at the first check where the answer reveals an incomplete stage — that is your current working edge.

---

### Stage 1 Check — Affections Mapped

**Question:** In the area of life where you feel the most internal pressure right now, what are you actually trusting for security, significance, or identity — not what you believe you *should* trust, but what you are functionally depending on?

> *(Name it as specifically as possible — not "approval" but "I need [specific person/outcome] to respond a certain way before I feel okay." Not "success" but "I need [specific thing] to feel like I am not a failure.")*
>
>

**What is that dependency providing — security, significance, or identity? What would its loss cost you?**

> *(The cost is the tell. Name what would be true about you if this dependency were removed.)*
>
>

**Diagnostic check — all three must be present to proceed:**

- [ ] I can name the specific dependency — not a general category, but the particular thing I am trusting right now
- [ ] I can name what it is providing (security / significance / identity)
- [ ] I can name what its loss would cost me — and feel the weight of that

**If any box is unchecked:** You are in Stage 1. The work is affections discovery — surfacing what you are actually trusting with enough specificity to name the false identity in Stage 2. See [[Covenant Identity — Affections Discovery — In-Session Protocol]].

**If all three are checked:** Proceed to Stage 2 Check.

---

### Stage 2 Check — False Identity Named

**Question:** What does the affections picture reveal about who you believe you are? Name the identity-level lie — not a behavior to change, but the "I am only _____ when _____" or "Without _____, I am _____" statement the dependency is organized around.

> *(Write the lie in its own voice — the way it sounds inside, not a cleaned-up version. The more precisely named, the more useful the rest of this document becomes.)*
>
>

**When you name it, what happens?**

> *(Describe your actual response — what you notice in your body, your emotional state, your instinct to argue with or agree with the statement. Do not describe what you think should happen.)*
>
>

**Is there weariness, grief, or fatigue directed at the lie itself — not at yourself, but at how long this pattern has been running?**

> *(Not guilt. Not self-condemnation. Something closer to: "I am tired of this still being true." The explanation-loop has quieted; there is less articulation, not more.)*
>
>

**Diagnostic check:**

- [ ] The lie is named at the identity level ("I am only..." / "Without..., I am..."), not at the behavioral level ("I tend to..." / "I struggle with...")
- [ ] Naming it produces emotional contact — a felt response in the body, not only intellectual recognition
- [ ] Something of weariness or grief is present toward the structure itself — the lie is not just identified, it is felt as failing

**If boxes 1–2 are unchecked:** Stage 2 is incomplete — the lie may be partially visible but is not yet named precisely at the identity level, or is named without emotional contact. Stay in Stage 1 work; the affections picture needs more depth before Stage 2 can close.

**If boxes 1–2 checked but box 3 unchecked:** Stage 2 is partially complete — the false structure is named and felt, but not yet experienced as inadequate. The lie has been seen but not yet grieved. Do not introduce covenant truth at this point — truth spoken before the false structure is felt as failing produces a proposition the mind holds alongside the lie rather than a truth that displaces it.

**If all three are checked:** Stage 2 is complete. Proceed to Stage 3 Check.

---

### Stage 3 Check — Covenant Truth Engagement

Speak covenant truth directly against the named lie. Not a general truth, but one addressed specifically to the false identity you named above — the counter-statement the lie most needs to hear.

**What happens when you hold the covenant truth?**

> *(Describe your actual response — not what should happen. What actually occurs when the truth is spoken.)*
>
>

**Locate your response in one of the three patterns below:**

- [ ] **A — Felt movement:** The truth lands with some resonance — a shift, a warmth, an exhale, even a partial sense that it reaches the place where the lie lives. You do not fully feel it yet, but something moves.

- [ ] **B — Active resistance:** The truth is engaged but pushback arrives — you find yourself explaining why it doesn't quite apply to your specific situation, articulating a counter-belief, or cycling back to the false identity through counter-reasoning. A particular belief appears to be blocking reception.

- [ ] **C — Cognitive receipt, felt gap:** You receive the truth as correct — you believe it is true — but it produces no felt movement. The lie does not feel argued-down; the truth simply does not reach where the lie lives. Knowing and feeling are disconnected.

**If A is checked:** You are in Stage 3 — stay here and deepen. The truth is reaching; the work is repetition in an emotionally live state, not new content. Proceed to Part 2.

**If B is checked:** Move to Stage 4a Check.

**If C is checked:** Move to Stage 4b Check.

---

### Stage 4a Check — Cognitive Distortion Blocking

**What specific belief is blocking you from receiving covenant truth?**

> *(Name it as precisely as possible — not "I just don't fully believe it" but "I believe this instead: [specific counter-belief]." The more specifically named, the more tractable it is.)*
>
>

**What is the argument the blocking belief makes — the case it presents for why the covenant truth does not fully apply to you?**

> *(Write the argument in its own voice. Let it speak before you try to answer it.)*
>
>

**When you follow the logic of the blocking belief all the way down — what does it require you to conclude about yourself, about God, or about how the Father operates?**

> *(This is the downward arrow: "And if that were true, what would that mean?" Follow it until you reach the load-bearing assumption.)*
>
>

**Current location:** Stage 4a — cognitive distortion blocking covenant truth reception.

**Next step:** Bring the blocking belief to the [[Lie Eliminator - Personal|Lie Eliminator]] (it may need its own entry), use Socratic probing to examine the evidence and logic beneath it, or bring it to a coaching session. The goal is not to win an argument but to loosen the distortion enough to return to Stage 3 — to make space for the covenant truth to reach what the distortion has been protecting.

---

### Stage 4b Check — Implicit Memory Barrier

**Describe the gap between knowing and feeling as precisely as you can.**

> *(What does it feel like to know something is true and not be able to feel it as operative? Where does the gap live — in your body, in your response to the word, in the distance between what you say and what you actually experience?)*
>
>

**How long has this particular truth been received cognitively without reaching the felt level?**

> *(A recent disconnection is different from a decade-long pattern. Both are valid; the duration changes what is needed.)*
>
>

**Current location:** Stage 4b — implicit memory barrier present.

**What this needs:** Not more argument, more precision, or better theology. The implicit level does not respond to additional content — it has already received what the explicit mind can hold. What it responds to:
- Repeated return to the covenant truth while the lie is emotionally live — not when you are settled and calm
- Relational encounter with someone who holds the truth for you across sessions — because implicit memory shifts through accumulated relational experience, not single-session insight
- Slow, attentive presence with the gap itself — naming the distance between knowing and feeling, without trying to close it through effort

The practitioner reference for this stage is [[Covenant Identity — Stage 4b Implicit Level Practitioner Reference]].

## Part 2 — Telos Profile

The telos qualities are not entry conditions for the arc — they are what becomes possible as the arc progresses. Rate each quality honestly: **Present** (operative in your actual experience, not only in your best moments), **Emerging** (beginning to show in some contexts but not yet stable), or **Largely Absent** (rarely or never operative at a felt level).

| Interior Quality | What it looks like from the inside | Rating |
|---|---|---|
| **Settled** | Groundedness that does not depend on circumstances resolving — not moved from the root even when things are unresolved or misunderstood | Present / Emerging / Largely Absent |
| **Undivided** | Fully present in this moment, with this person — not split between what is happening and what might happen or what happened earlier | Present / Emerging / Largely Absent |
| **Free** | Interior not owned by what others think, not hostage to outcomes, not shrunk by fear of what might be said or taken away | Present / Emerging / Largely Absent |
| **Moved Without Being Swept** | Fully inhabited emotional life that stays anchored — you can feel deeply without losing access to yourself | Present / Emerging / Largely Absent |
| **Unhurried** | No background anxiety about what is not being accomplished; the person in front of you is the thing, not a pause in the mission | Present / Emerging / Largely Absent |
| **Loved and Knowing It** | Not believing it — *knowing* it, operating from it as a lived fact rather than an aspiration | Present / Emerging / Largely Absent |
| **Truthful Without Cruelty** | Seeing and speaking clearly — the love and the truth arrive at the same time; neither softened into uselessness nor wielded as a weapon | Present / Emerging / Largely Absent |
| **Purposeful Without Striving** | Knowing what you are for, expressing it — not doing the work and simultaneously proving you deserve to be doing it | Present / Emerging / Largely Absent |
| **Available** | Interior facing outward before the encounter begins — stable enough to receive another person's state without flinching or being overwhelmed by it | Present / Emerging / Largely Absent |

**What do you observe in the telos profile?**

> *(Which qualities are clustered together as Present or Absent? What pattern do you see? The clustering often names what the arc work has produced so far — and what it most needs next.)*

## Quick Reference — Stage Locations and Next Steps

| Location | What is true here | Next step |
|---|---|---|
| **Stage 1** | Affections unmapped — the specific dependency cannot be named with precision | Affections discovery; deepen with [[Covenant Identity — Affections Discovery — In-Session Protocol]] |
| **Stage 1→2 threshold** | Affections mapped; false identity named but without emotional contact or felt inadequacy of the false structure | Stay in Stage 1 work; do not introduce covenant truth — truth spoken before Stage 2 closes produces a parallel proposition, not displacement |
| **Stage 2 complete** | False structure named with emotional contact and felt as failing | Introduce covenant truth directly against the named false identity; verify the implicit belief is emotionally live before speaking |
| **Stage 3 active** | Covenant truth landing with some felt movement | Stay here; deepen with declaration, Scripture saturation, repeated receptive return |
| **Stage 4a** | Active cognitive distortion blocking covenant truth reception | Name the blocking belief precisely; [[Lie Eliminator - Personal\|Lie Eliminator]] or coaching session |
| **Stage 4b** | Implicit barrier — truth received cognitively, felt gap present | Relational encounter across time; not more content; [[Covenant Identity — Stage 4b Implicit Level Practitioner Reference]] |
| **Telos emerging** | Interior qualities becoming operative in actual experience | Spiritual disciplines, formation practices, between-session work; which qualities are absent names what the arc has not yet reached |

## Document Provenance

**What this is:** A self-locating diagnostic for the covenant identity arc — the practitioner's navigational logic from [[Covenant Identity — Diagnostic Lens Transition Logic]] converted into a fillable self-assessment form. Where the Transition Logic answers *"what am I seeing in this client and where do I go next?"*, this document answers *"where am I right now, and what does that tell me about what I need?"*

**Scope boundary:** This document produces a location, not a prescription. The stage location tells you where you are and what is true about that location — it does not tell you how to execute the work of each stage. Execution references are linked at each stage. For use in a coaching engagement, this document can orient pre-session preparation; it does not replace the in-session diagnostic sequence.

**Upstream documents:** [[Covenant Identity — Diagnostic Lens Transition Logic]]; [[Covenant Identity — The Interior Life as Telos]]

**Client use note:** Stages 1–2 should be administered in session rather than assigned for solo completion — affections disclosure and false identity naming require the relational container of a live coaching conversation. Parts 2 and 3 can be assigned between sessions once Stage 2 is complete.
