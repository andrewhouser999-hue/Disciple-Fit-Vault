**Disciple Fit Coaching | Andrew Houser**
*Covenant Identity Coaching Engagement*

---

*This agreement is between Andrew Houser, operating as Disciple Fit Coaching ("Coach"), and the undersigned client ("Client"). Please read it in full before signing. Questions are welcome before or at the start of session 1.*

---

## 1. What This Coaching Is — and Is Not

Covenant Identity Coaching is a faith-based life coaching engagement. It is not therapy, counseling, psychiatric treatment, or pastoral care. The Coach is not a licensed mental health professional, physician, or ordained minister.

Coaching works in the Gap between the Client's declared covenant identity in Christ and their lived experience of it. It is appropriate for emotionally functional adults navigating formation issues, identity patterns, and spiritual formation blocks.

Coaching does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. If clinical material surfaces during the engagement that exceeds coaching scope — including but not limited to active depression, trauma requiring clinical processing, suicidal ideation, or self-harm — the Coach will name this directly and support the Client in connecting with appropriate clinical care.

---

## 2. Faith Integration

This coaching engagement is explicitly Christian in framework and practice. The Coach operates from a biblically-grounded model in which Scripture is authoritative and the work of formation is ultimately the Holy Spirit's. Sessions may include prayer and reference to Scripture. The Client is entering a coaching relationship with a faith-integrated character — not a secular life coaching relationship with optional spiritual content.

The Coach conducts this engagement in alignment with the International Coaching Federation (ICF) Code of Ethics.

## 5. Cancellation Policy

**Session cancellation:** The Client must provide at least 24 hours advance notice to cancel or reschedule a session.

**Missed sessions:** A session cancelled with less than 24 hours notice or not attended without notice may be rescheduled within 3 calendar days at the Coach's availability. If rescheduling within 3 days is not possible, the session will be applied to the next scheduled session date. Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice more than twice in a single engagement will not be eligible for reschedule and will be forfeited.

**Coach cancellation:** If the Coach must cancel a session, it will be rescheduled at no penalty to the Client at the next available time.

---

## 6. Refund and Early Termination Policy

**Before intake form submission:** Full refund of the engagement fee, less any payment processing fees.

**After intake form submission, before session 1:** Refund of the engagement fee minus $145 (the intake component), less any payment processing fees.

**After session 1:** The engagement fee is non-refundable. If the Client chooses to end the engagement early, unused sessions may be applied as credit toward a future Disciple Fit Coaching engagement, valid for 12 months from the date of the final session attended.

**Coach-initiated termination:** If the Coach determines that the Client's needs exceed the scope of coaching and the Client declines referral to appropriate clinical care, the Coach reserves the right to terminate the engagement. In this case, unused sessions will be refunded in full.

Either party may end the engagement with written notice. The Client is not obligated to continue and will not be penalized for choosing to stop beyond the refund terms above.

---

## 7. Growth Documentation

The Coach maintains records of this engagement to support the Client's growth and to build the growth documents that are a core component of this model. These records include:

- **Intake Addendum** — baseline documentation completed by the Coach after the intake form and session 1
- **Session Growth Notes** — completed by the Coach within 24 hours of each session
- **Mid-Engagement Snapshot** — prepared by the Coach and presented to the Client at approximately session 4–5
- **Growth Report** — prepared by the Coach and presented to the Client at or following the final session

The Client's identifying information is stored using a privacy code system. The Client's name and code are maintained separately from session content. Session content and growth documents are kept confidential and will not be shared with third parties without the Client's written consent, except as required by law or as necessary to protect the safety of the Client or others (see Section 8).

The Growth Report and Mid-Engagement Snapshot belong to the Client. The Client may request copies of these documents at any time.

---

## 8. Confidentiality and Its Limits

What the Client shares in sessions is held in confidence. The Coach will not disclose session content to third parties without the Client's written consent, with the following exceptions:

- **Safety:** If the Coach has reasonable concern that the Client is at risk of harm to themselves or others, the Coach may take action to protect safety, including contacting emergency services or a designated contact person.
- **Legal requirement:** If the Coach is required by applicable law to disclose information, the Coach will do so to the minimum extent required.
- **Supervision:** The Coach may discuss session content with a coaching supervisor or consultant for the purpose of professional development. Client identity will not be disclosed in these conversations.

The Client is encouraged to inform the Coach at the start of the engagement of any person who should be contacted in case of emergency.

---

## 9. Not a Crisis Service

Coaching sessions are not a crisis resource. If the Client is experiencing a psychiatric emergency, is having thoughts of suicide or self-harm, or is in immediate danger, the Client should contact:

- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text **988**
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text HOME to **741741**
- **Emergency services:** **911**

The Coach is available for scheduled sessions and reasonable between-session communication. The Coach is not available for after-hours crisis support and cannot guarantee response time to urgent messages outside of scheduled sessions.

---

## 10. Referral

The Coach may recommend that the Client connect with a licensed mental health professional, physician, or pastoral counselor at any point during the engagement. This recommendation is a professional judgment about appropriate care — not a judgment about the Client's willingness, faith, or character. The Client is not obligated to follow a referral recommendation, but the Coach reserves the right to pause or end the engagement if clinical material is present that coaching cannot responsibly hold.
