# Disciple Fit — Market Segment Index

**Purpose:** Master reference for all defined, partially defined, and hypothetical market segments within the Disciple Fit scope. One segment is the current target market. Others are adjacent or future.

**Rule:** Only one segment carries Target Market status at a time. New segments are added as Hypothetical until profiled. No segment gets equal marketing weight until the target segment is validated.

## Status Definitions

- **Defined** — Full profile complete: persona, problems, trigger, reach, trust
- **Partially Defined** — Persona built, reach/trigger/trust layer incomplete
- **Hypothetical** — Named and described, not yet profiled

## Target Market

**Current:** The Sunday-Monday Christian

> High-functioning, theologically literate Christian. Well-off. Performs well across every role. Feels the widening gap between their Sunday self and Monday self. Has knowledge without identity, community without intimacy, and faith without the embodied self to inhabit it. Benefits from coaching + CBT + Biblical framework simultaneously.
