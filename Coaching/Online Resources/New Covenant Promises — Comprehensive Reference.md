# New Covenant Promises of God — Comprehensive Reference

## Foundation Texts
The New Covenant is formally announced in **Jeremiah 31:31–34**, **Ezekiel 36:25–27**, and inaugurated by Christ in **Luke 22:20** / **Hebrews 8–10**.

---

## I. Forgiveness & Justification

- **Complete forgiveness of sin** — "I will forgive their iniquity, and I will remember their sin no more." (Jer. 31:34)
- **Justification by faith, not works** — declared righteous through Christ's righteousness imputed to us (Rom. 3:21–26; 5:1)
- **No condemnation** — "There is therefore now no condemnation for those who are in Christ Jesus." (Rom. 8:1)
- **Redemption through his blood** — "In him we have redemption through his blood, the forgiveness of our trespasses." (Eph. 1:7)
- **Cleansing from all unrighteousness** — "If we confess our sins, he is faithful and just to forgive us our sins and to cleanse us from all unrighteousness." (1 John 1:9)
- **Sins removed as far as east from west** (Ps. 103:12 — fulfilled in covenant context)

---

## II. The Holy Spirit

- **Indwelling Spirit** — "I will put my Spirit within you." (Ezek. 36:27)
- **New heart and new spirit** — "I will give you a new heart and put a new spirit in you; I will remove your heart of stone and give you a heart of flesh." (Ezek. 36:26)
- **Spirit as seal and guarantee** — "sealed with the promised Holy Spirit, who is the guarantee of our inheritance." (Eph. 1:13–14)
- **Spirit as helper/advocate** — "I will ask the Father, and he will give you another Helper, to be with you forever." (John 14:16)
- **Spirit of adoption** — "You have received the Spirit of adoption as sons, by whom we cry, 'Abba! Father!'" (Rom. 8:15)
- **Spirit interceding for us** — "the Spirit himself intercedes for us with groanings too deep for words." (Rom. 8:26)
- **Spirit producing fruit in us** — love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, self-control (Gal. 5:22–23)
- **Spiritual gifts distributed** — "To each is given the manifestation of the Spirit for the common good." (1 Cor. 12:7)

---

## III. Transformation & Sanctification

- **Law written on the heart** — "I will put my law within them, and I will write it on their hearts." (Jer. 31:33)
- **Progressive conformation to Christ's image** — "predestined to be conformed to the image of his Son." (Rom. 8:29)
- **God working in you to will and to act** — "it is God who works in you, both to will and to work for his good pleasure." (Phil. 2:13)
- **Complete sanctification promised** — "the God of peace himself sanctify you completely." (1 Thess. 5:23)
- **Renewal of the mind** — "be transformed by the renewal of your mind." (Rom. 12:2)
- **Good works prepared in advance** — "we are his workmanship, created in Christ Jesus for good works, which God prepared beforehand." (Eph. 2:10)

---

## IV. Adoption & Belonging

- **Adoption as sons and daughters** — "I will be a father to you, and you shall be sons and daughters to me." (2 Cor. 6:18)
- **Co-heirs with Christ** — "heirs of God and fellow heirs with Christ." (Rom. 8:17)
- **Citizenship in heaven** — "our citizenship is in heaven." (Phil. 3:20)
- **Membership in the household of God** — "you are fellow citizens with the saints and members of the household of God." (Eph. 2:19)
- **Known by God** — "the Lord knows those who are his." (2 Tim. 2:19)

---

## V. Access & Relationship with God

- **Direct, personal knowledge of God** — "they shall all know me, from the least of them to the greatest." (Jer. 31:34)
- **Access to the Father** — "through him we both have access in one Spirit to the Father." (Eph. 2:18)
- **Christ as mediator** — "there is one mediator between God and men, the man Christ Jesus." (1 Tim. 2:5)
- **God dwelling among his people** — "I will make my dwelling among them and walk among them." (2 Cor. 6:16)
- **Presence in prayer** — "draw near to God, and he will draw near to you." (James 4:8)
- **Hearing and answering prayer** — "If you ask me anything in my name, I will do it." (John 14:14)

---

## VI. Security & Preservation

- **Eternal security / preservation of the saints** — "no one will snatch them out of my hand." (John 10:28–29)
- **God completing what he started** — "he who began a good work in you will bring it to completion at the day of Jesus Christ." (Phil. 1:6)
- **Nothing separating from God's love** — (Rom. 8:38–39)
- **Kept by God's power** — "who by God's power are being guarded through faith for a salvation ready to be revealed." (1 Pet. 1:5)
- **Christ interceding for us always** — "he always lives to make intercession for them." (Heb. 7:25)
- **God's faithfulness in temptation** — "he will not let you be tempted beyond your ability, but with the temptation he will also provide the way of escape." (1 Cor. 10:13)

---

## VII. Provision & Care

- **All needs supplied** — "my God will supply every need of yours according to his riches in glory in Christ Jesus." (Phil. 4:19)
- **Sovereign working of all things for good** — "for those who love God all things work together for good." (Rom. 8:28)
- **Freedom from anxiety** — "cast all your anxieties on him, because he cares for you." (1 Pet. 5:7)
- **Peace beyond understanding** — "the peace of God, which surpasses all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds." (Phil. 4:7)
- **Wisdom given generously** — "If any of you lacks wisdom, let him ask God, who gives generously to all without reproach." (James 1:5)
- **Strength for every season** — "I can do all things through him who strengthens me." (Phil. 4:13)
- **Rest** — "Come to me, all who labor and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest." (Matt. 11:28)

---

## VIII. Resurrection & Eternal Life

- **Eternal life** — "whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life." (John 3:16)
- **Resurrection of the body** — "he who raised Christ Jesus from the dead will also give life to your mortal bodies." (Rom. 8:11)
- **Glorification** — "those whom he justified he also glorified." (Rom. 8:30)
- **Inheritance that cannot perish** — "an inheritance that is imperishable, undefiled, and unfading." (1 Pet. 1:4)
- **Sharing in Christ's glory** — "we shall also reign with him." (2 Tim. 2:12)
- **New creation** — "Behold, I am making all things new." (Rev. 21:5)
- **No more death, mourning, crying, or pain** (Rev. 21:4)

---

## IX. Mission & Authority

- **All authority backing the mission** — "All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me." (Matt. 28:18)
- **Christ's presence in mission** — "I am with you always, to the end of the age." (Matt. 28:20)
- **Fruitfulness** — "I appointed you that you should go and bear fruit and that your fruit should abide." (John 15:16)
- **Greater works** — "whoever believes in me will also do the works that I do; and greater works than these will he do." (John 14:12)

---

## X. The Unilateral Nature of the Covenant

A crucial distinctive of the New Covenant versus the Mosaic: **God promises what he himself will do**, not conditions dependent on human performance. The "I will" structure in Jeremiah 31 and Ezekiel 36 is entirely God's initiative. Obedience is the *fruit* of the covenant, not its condition.

> "This is the covenant I will make with the house of Israel after those days, declares the Lord: I will put my law within them, and I will write it on their hearts. And I will be their God, and they shall be my people." — Jer. 31:33
