# Employment LeaderComm Notes

**Here is how I am a Consultant, employee**
Leadership does not respect task completers. They respect problem solvers. 
Think like a strategic consultant. They don't ask for permission, they bring a point of view.
- Identify what the Executive's real need? Not what they say they want. What problem keeps them up at night?
- What does success look like from their perspective?
- What makes my approach uniquely valuable? Why should they trust me over others. 
This means owning the work, leading with a narrative and taking extreme ownership. Think of it not as reporting up but as partnering across.
