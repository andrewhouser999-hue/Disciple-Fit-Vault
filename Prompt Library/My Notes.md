  *"For thus says the One who is high and lifted up, who inhabits eternity, whose name is Holy: 'I dwell in the high and holy place, and also with him who is of a contrite and lowly spirit, to revive the spirit of the lowly, and to revive the heart of the contrite.'"*
  
  This is the answer to my prayer for God to take care of the part of me that will not obey him, follow him where it counts.

*Isn't it better being made in your inner parts as a King without the honor of the robe, than to be wearing Kingly garments, unfit to be a King?* Andrew Houser
1. The poor in Spirit, the crushed, and contrite have the ranking stripes that begin to qualify them as Kings. 
2. These in His Spirit are the ones who will look out over their kingdom and see what is needed by their people to thrive. 
3. These are the ones who will be built up in the Beatitudes. Unless one is willing to lay down their life, they are corruptible.
