# A Letter to the People I Love

---

**To my family and friends,**

I'm writing this letter because I love you. Not the kind of love that stays quiet to keep the peace. The kind that speaks up when something urgent needs to be said — even when it's uncomfortable, even when it sounds strange, even when you might roll your eyes or set this letter down.

Please don't set it down.

What I'm about to share with you is the most important thing I have ever communicated to anyone. I'm not writing this from a place of fear or fanaticism. I'm writing it from a place of deep conviction, after years of reading the Bible carefully and watching the world closely — and realizing that the two are lining up in ways that should stop every serious person cold.

I'm not asking you to take my word for anything. I'm asking you to read, look at the evidence, and make your own judgment. The stakes are your eternal life. That deserves more than a quick dismissal.

---

## PART ONE: THE BOOK THAT SAW OUR WORLD COMING

The Bible was written over roughly 1,500 years by approximately 40 authors across three continents. It contains more than 2,500 prophecies — specific, detailed predictions about nations, kings, empires, and the future of humanity. By any statistical measure, the fulfillment of these prophecies is not explainable by coincidence or good guessing.

One of the most stunning: the nation of Israel.

In 70 A.D., the Roman Empire destroyed Jerusalem, burned the Temple, and scattered the Jewish people to the four corners of the earth. That should have been the end of them as a distinct people — it was the end of every other people group Rome conquered and dispersed. But the Bible predicted, centuries before it happened, that Israel would be scattered *and then regathered* to their land in the last days.

> *"I will bring them back from captivity and have compassion on them."* — Jeremiah 30:3

> *"Who has ever heard of such things? Who has ever seen things like this? Can a country be born in a day or a nation be brought forth in a moment?"* — Isaiah 66:8

On **May 14, 1948**, after nearly 1,900 years of dispersion, Israel was reborn as a nation — in a single day. The world watched it happen. This was not a vague, interpretable prediction. It was specific, dramatic, and historically unprecedented.

Jesus himself pointed to Israel as the clock of the end times:

> *"Now learn this lesson from the fig tree: As soon as its twigs get tender and its leaves come out, you know that summer is near. Even so, when you see all these things, you know that it is near, right at the door."* — Matthew 24:32-33

The fig tree is Israel. It budded in 1948. The clock started.

## PART TWO: THE SIGNS — AND WHY RIGHT NOW IS DIFFERENT

You may be thinking: *people have always said the end is near.* That's true. And the Bible even predicted that response:

> *"Above all, you must understand that in the last days scoffers will come, scoffing and following their own evil desires. They will say, 'Where is this coming he promised? Ever since our ancestors died, everything goes on as it has since the beginning of creation.'"* — 2 Peter 3:3-4

The difference today is not one sign. It's the convergence of virtually every sign Jesus and the prophets listed — simultaneously, for the first time in history.

In Matthew 24, Jesus was asked directly: *What will be the sign of your coming and the end of the age?* His answer:

> *"You will hear of wars and rumors of wars... Nation will rise against nation, and kingdom against kingdom. There will be famines and earthquakes in various places. All these are the beginning of birth pains."* — Matthew 24:6-8

Birth pains. Meaning they increase in frequency and intensity as the moment approaches.

**Now look at the world we actually live in:**

### "Peace and Safety" — The Phrase the Bible Predicted Would Define the Moment Before Destruction

This is one of the most precise and verifiable signs in all of prophetic Scripture, and it is playing out on your television screen constantly.

> *"While people are saying, 'Peace and safety,' destruction will come on them suddenly, as labor pains on a pregnant woman, and they will not escape."* — 1 Thessalonians 5:3

Read that carefully. The apostle Paul, writing nearly 2,000 years ago, identified the exact cultural atmosphere that would exist immediately before sudden, catastrophic judgment: a world loudly, repeatedly, almost reflexively declaring "peace and safety" — right up until the moment it collapses.

Now look at the world we actually live in.

**The phrase "peace and security" has become the single most repeated phrase in international diplomacy** — and its use has accelerated sharply in recent years to a degree that has no parallel in prior decades.

Consider what has happened just in recent years:

- The **United Nations General Assembly** — every September, every head of state takes the podium. If you watch those speeches, the phrase "peace and security" appears so frequently it has become background noise. In the 2024 session, virtually every address — from small nations to global superpowers — included it. The UN's own charter uses it as a foundational phrase, but the *intensity* and *desperation* of its use has increased dramatically as actual world stability has deteriorated.

- **NATO's communiqués** from their 2023 and 2024 summits used "peace and security" as a central organizing phrase — even as member nations simultaneously increased military spending to the highest levels since the Cold War. Leaders were calling for peace while preparing for war, simultaneously, without apparent awareness of the contradiction.

- The **Ukraine Peace Summit** held in Switzerland in June 2024 drew representatives from over 90 nations — one of the largest peace conferences in modern history — specifically convened to call for peace in a war that has killed hundreds of thousands of people and shows no sign of ending.

- **U.S. Secretary of State Antony Blinken** made more diplomatic trips to the Middle East between October 2023 and early 2025 than arguably any Secretary of State in history over a comparable period — each trip centered on calls for ceasefire, peace deals, and regional stability. The word "ceasefire" was spoken by world leaders more times in 2023-2024 than in any prior period in recorded history.

- **Pope Francis** has issued public calls for peace in Ukraine, Gaza, Sudan, Myanmar, and multiple other conflicts — with a frequency and urgency that is historically unusual even for a pope.

- **António Guterres**, the UN Secretary-General, warned in 2023 that the world was facing its most dangerous moment since World War II — *while simultaneously calling for peace.* He has used the phrase "peace and security" in public statements so frequently that his office has essentially made it a signature declaration.

- The **Abraham Accords (2020)** normalized relations between Israel and four Arab nations — UAE, Bahrain, Sudan, and Morocco — and set off the largest wave of Middle East peace diplomacy in decades. Saudi-Israel normalization talks advanced significantly in 2023-2024, with the explicit framing of achieving a "historic peace."

- The **Biden administration**, the **Trump administration**, the **EU**, the **Arab League**, and the **African Union** have all — simultaneously and repeatedly — been calling for peace and a two-state solution in the Middle East. The convergence of global voices all crying "peace" is unprecedented.

Here is what makes this so striking: this explosion of peace rhetoric has not coincided with an era of actual peace. It has coincided with the opposite — the most dangerous, unstable global environment since World War II, by the assessment of military analysts, nuclear risk monitors, and world leaders themselves. The Bulletin of Atomic Scientists moved the **Doomsday Clock to 89 seconds to midnight in January 2024** — the closest to midnight it has ever been in its 77-year history.

The world is simultaneously shouting "peace and safety" while its own experts say we are closer to catastrophic war than ever before.

That is exactly what 1 Thessalonians 5:3 described. Not a world that has achieved peace. A world that is *crying* for it — loudly, desperately, repeatedly — right before sudden destruction arrives.

The Bible also gives us a preview of how peace will be used as a weapon in the Tribulation itself. The Antichrist's rise to power begins with a peace agreement:

> *"He will confirm a covenant with many for one 'seven.'"* — Daniel 9:27

> *"When they feel secure, he will destroy many."* — Daniel 8:25

> *"By peace he shall destroy many."* — Daniel 8:25 (KJV)

The pattern is this: false peace, manufactured security, then destruction. We are watching the stage being set for exactly that sequence — in real time.

## PART TWO-B: MORE SIGNS THE BIBLE SAW COMING

Beyond what has already been described, the Bible gives a detailed list of specific conditions and events that would characterize the generation approaching the Tribulation. What follows is a systematic comparison. Look at it honestly.

---

### The Euphrates River Will Dry Up

> *"The sixth angel poured out his bowl on the great river Euphrates, and its water was dried up to prepare the way for the kings from the East."* — Revelation 16:12

The Euphrates River — one of the great rivers of the ancient world, running through modern Turkey, Syria, and Iraq — is **drying up.** This is not metaphor or exaggeration. It is documented, measured, and reported:

- Since **2011 alone**, the Euphrates has lost **75% of its water flow** — a collapse so severe that what remains in many stretches barely resembles the river it once was. And that figure does not include the significant reductions that occurred in prior decades from upstream dam construction.
- Iraq's Ministry of Water Resources has publicly warned that the Euphrates could effectively disappear entirely without dramatic and immediate intervention.
- Turkey's massive upstream dam projects — including the Atatürk Dam — have dramatically choked the river's flow before it ever reaches Syria or Iraq.
- In 2021 and 2022, water levels dropped so severely that ancient archaeological sites submerged for decades were exposed on the riverbed.
- Iraqi farmers along the Euphrates have abandoned their land in large numbers due to complete loss of usable water.

Revelation was written approximately 95 A.D. The Euphrates drying up was, for most of history, an unimaginable prediction about one of the most reliably flowing rivers in the ancient world — a river so central to civilization that the Garden of Eden is described as being near it (Genesis 2:14). It is drying up now, rapidly, measurably, within our lifetime.

---

### Damascus Will Become a Heap of Ruins

> *"See, Damascus will no longer be a city but will become a heap of ruins."* — Isaiah 17:1

Damascus, the capital of Syria, is believed to be the **oldest continuously inhabited city in the world** — occupied for over 10,000 years, never fully destroyed in all of recorded history. Until now, this prophecy had never been fulfilled.

The Syrian civil war, which began in 2011, brought heavy bombardment to Damascus and surrounding areas. Large sections of Syrian cities were reduced to rubble. The Assad regime fell suddenly in December 2024 after over 13 years of conflict, leaving Syria in a state of near-total fragmentation. The infrastructure of the country — including areas of Damascus — has been catastrophically damaged. This prophecy is not yet fully fulfilled, but for the first time in thousands of years, the conditions for its fulfillment are visibly present.

---

### Knowledge and Travel Will Explode

> *"But you, Daniel, close up and seal the words of the scroll until the time of the end. Many will go here and there to increase knowledge."* — Daniel 12:4

For virtually all of human history, the speed at which people traveled and information moved was limited by horses and wind. Then, in roughly 200 years:

- **Global air travel:** In 2023, approximately **4.5 billion passengers** flew on commercial aircraft. The Wright Brothers flew for the first time in 1903.
- **The internet:** As of 2024, over **5.4 billion people** — two-thirds of the world's population — have access to the internet. The sum total of human knowledge is accessible to a person on a device that fits in their pocket.
- **Artificial Intelligence:** In 2022-2023, AI systems surpassed human performance in legal, medical, and academic benchmarks. The speed of knowledge accumulation has become effectively impossible to track.

No generation in all of history prior to ours has seen knowledge increase at anything remotely approaching this speed. Daniel described it precisely.

### The Gospel Will Be Preached to Every Nation

> *"And this gospel of the kingdom will be preached in the whole world as a testimony to all nations, and then the end will come."* — Matthew 24:14

Jesus said this was a prerequisite for the end. For most of history, vast portions of humanity had never heard the name of Jesus Christ. Today:

- Of the approximately **7,300 known living languages** in the world, only **544 still need a Bible translation** — meaning active translation work is ongoing or yet to begin. That number continues to shrink.
- The Bible has already been translated into enough languages to reach the vast majority of the world's population in their native tongue.
- Christian radio, satellite television, and the internet have carried the Gospel into nations where physical missionaries cannot go — including closed countries like Iran, North Korea, and Saudi Arabia, where underground church movements are growing despite severe persecution.
- Christianity is the world's largest religion, with approximately **2.4 billion adherents** across every nation on earth.

We are closer to this prophecy being fully completed than any prior generation. Its fulfillment is a stated precondition for the end. The finish line is visible.

> *"There will be signs in the sun, moon and stars. On the earth, nations will be in anguish and perplexity at the roaring and tossing of the sea."* — Luke 21:25

> *"The sun will be turned to darkness and the moon to blood before the coming of the great and dreadful day of the Lord."* — Joel 2:31

- In **May 2024**, Earth experienced the most powerful geomagnetic storm in **21 years** — a G5-level event caused by solar flares. The aurora borealis was visible as far south as Florida and Texas — places that essentially never see it.
- We are currently at or near **solar maximum** — the peak of the sun's 11-year activity cycle — meaning solar flares, coronal mass ejections, and disruptions to Earth's electromagnetic environment are at their highest point.
- Between 2014 and 2015, four consecutive **blood moon** lunar eclipses (a tetrad) fell on Jewish feast days — Passover and the Feast of Tabernacles in both years. This specific alignment has occurred only a handful of times in the last 500 years and has historically corresponded to significant events involving Israel.

## PART THREE: WHAT IS COMING — AND WHAT IT MEANS FOR YOU

Here is the sequence the Bible describes for the events ahead. I want to be clear: there are sincere Christians who disagree on some of the specific timing and details. But the events themselves are described plainly in Scripture.

---

### The Rapture

The Bible teaches that before the worst of what is coming, Jesus Christ will come to collect his people — those who have genuinely trusted in him for salvation. This is called the Rapture.

> *"For the Lord himself will come down from heaven, with a loud command, with the voice of the archangel and with the trumpet call of God, and the dead in Christ will rise first. After that, we who are still alive and are left will be caught up together with them in the clouds to meet the Lord in the air. And so we will be with the Lord forever."* — 1 Thessalonians 4:16-17

> *"Listen, I tell you a mystery: We will not all sleep, but we will all be changed — in a flash, in the twinkling of an eye, at the last trumpet."* — 1 Corinthians 15:51-52

This will happen suddenly, without warning, to people across the globe. If you are reading this letter after that event has occurred and people around you have vanished — what follows in this letter is specifically for you.

---

### The Seven-Year Tribulation

Following the Rapture, the Bible describes a seven-year period of unprecedented global catastrophe called the Great Tribulation.

> *"For then there will be great distress, unequaled from the beginning of the world until now — and never to be equaled again."* — Matthew 24:21

This period is described in detail in the book of Revelation, chapters 6 through 19. It includes:

- **Seal Judgments** (Revelation 6): War, famine, death, and cosmic disruption on a global scale. A quarter of the world's population dies.
- **Trumpet Judgments** (Revelation 8-9): Ecological catastrophes — a third of the oceans, rivers, and vegetation destroyed. Supernatural darkness. Demonic activity unleashed.
- **Bowl Judgments** (Revelation 16): The final and most severe — painful sores on those who bear the mark, seas turning to blood, scorching heat, total darkness, the drying of the Euphrates River, and the largest earthquake in human history.

### The Antichrist and the Mark

During this period, a charismatic world leader will rise — the Bible calls him the Antichrist, the man of lawlessness, the beast. He will initially appear as a peacemaker, possibly brokering a peace agreement involving Israel (Daniel 9:27). Midway through the seven years, he will reveal his true nature — demanding worship and implementing the mark-based economic system described in Revelation 13.

**This is the most critical decision you will face:**

> *"If anyone worships the beast and its image and receives its mark on their forehead or on their hand, they, too, will drink the wine of God's fury, which has been poured full strength into the cup of his wrath."* — Revelation 14:9-10

Taking this mark — whatever form it takes — to buy food, access money, or preserve your life will be a conscious act of allegiance to a system that rejects God. The Bible is unambiguous: do not take it. The consequences of taking it are eternal. The consequences of refusing it will be severe — persecution, inability to participate in the economy, and possibly death. But those who endure and refuse the mark and place their faith in Christ will be saved.

> *"I saw thrones on which were seated those who had been given authority to judge. And I saw the souls of those who had been beheaded because of their testimony about Jesus and because of the word of God. They had not worshiped the beast or its image and had not received its mark on their foreheads or their hands. They came to life and reigned with Christ a thousand years."* — Revelation 20:4

The Bible says there will still be people who come to faith during the Tribulation. It will be the hardest road imaginable. But God does not abandon people even in that period.

> *"They will see the Son of Man coming on the clouds of heaven, with power and great glory."* — Matthew 24:30

> *"Look, he is coming with the clouds, and every eye will see him."* — Revelation 1:7

He defeats the Antichrist, ends the Tribulation, and establishes his kingdom on earth. Every person who has ever lived will ultimately face judgment.

> *"It is appointed for man to die once, and after that comes judgment."* — Hebrews 9:27

## PART FOUR: THE GOSPEL — THE ONLY THING THAT ACTUALLY MATTERS

Everything I've written above is meant to get your attention. But this section is the reason for the letter.

**The problem:** Every human being who has ever lived has violated the moral law of God. This isn't religious opinion — it's a truth you can verify in your own conscience. You know you've lied. You know you've been selfish, cruel, dishonest, or worse. The Bible calls this sin, and it has a consequence:

> *"For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God."* — Romans 3:23

> *"For the wages of sin is death."* — Romans 6:23

Not just physical death. Spiritual death — eternal separation from God. This is what the Bible calls hell, and Jesus spoke about it more than any other figure in Scripture. He did not speak about it to terrify people into compliance. He spoke about it because it is real and because he came specifically to rescue people from it.

**The solution:** God did not stand at a distance and watch humanity destroy itself. He entered his own creation.

> *"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."* — John 3:16

Jesus Christ — fully God and fully human — lived the perfect life none of us could live, then died on a Roman cross to absorb the penalty for sin that we deserved. He was buried, and three days later he rose from the dead — physically, bodily, historically. This is the central claim of Christianity and the event on which everything stands or falls.

> *"For what I received I passed on to you as of first importance: that Christ died for our sins according to the Scriptures, that he was buried, that he was raised on the third day according to the Scriptures, and that he appeared to Cephas, and then to the Twelve. After that, he appeared to more than five hundred of the brothers and sisters at the same time."* — 1 Corinthians 15:3-6

Five hundred eyewitnesses. This is not mythology. It was written within decades of the event, while eyewitnesses were still alive and could contradict it. They didn't.

**How to receive this:** Salvation is not earned by being good enough, attending church, or religious effort. It is received by faith.

> *"If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved."* — Romans 10:9

> *"For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith — and this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God — not by works, so that no one can boast."* — Ephesians 2:8-9

But genuine faith produces genuine repentance — a real turning away from sin. Not perfection. A direction change. A willingness to stop running from God and start running toward him.

> *"Repent, then, and turn to God, so that your sins may be wiped out."* — Acts 3:19

**What this means practically:**

You can pray right now. There is no magic formula. God sees your heart. But if you mean it, tell him:

*"God, I know I've sinned against you. I know I can't fix that on my own. I believe Jesus Christ died for my sins and rose from the dead. I turn from my sin and place my trust in him. I receive the forgiveness you offer. Make me yours."*

If you've prayed that and meant it — the Bible says you are saved. Not because of the prayer, but because of the God who heard it and the Christ whose death made it possible.

> *"Everyone who calls on the name of the Lord will be saved."* — Romans 10:13

## PART FIVE: IF YOU'RE READING THIS AFTER THE RAPTURE

If people around you have suddenly disappeared and this letter found its way into your hands — first, understand what happened. The event described in 1 Thessalonians 4 has occurred. Those who knew and trusted Jesus Christ have been caught up to be with him.

The chaos around you is the beginning of the Tribulation period. Here is what you need to know:

**1. It is not too late.** God's grace is still available during the Tribulation. People will come to faith during this period (Revelation 7:9-14). It will cost you everything in this world. But eternal life is worth infinitely more than physical survival in a collapsing world.
