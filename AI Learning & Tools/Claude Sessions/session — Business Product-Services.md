## Context
Andrew is building Disciple Fit — a faith-based coaching business using the CBC model (CBT + Coaching + Biblical Framework). The core methodology integrates 8 psychological modalities with Kingdom coaching (somatic-cognitive integration + Holy Spirit agency). Material built includes: Parable of the Soils diagnostic framework, Lie Eliminator (7-part format + lie archaeology + agreement audit + somatic anchoring), Identity Installer (22 identity categories, 7-part format), Comprehensive Discipleship Markers (15 categories), and a Diagnostic Intake Form (18 questions, tiered by diagnostic yield).

Demand for Disciple Fit is currently latent and unvalidated. One active client (SC-00r, Week 1 not yet run). No platform or audience yet. Target buyer: "stuck Christians" — people who know the truth but can't feel it or live from it.

## Current State
Full product and services evaluation completed under ruthless advisor framework.

**Products evaluated:**
- Online Diagnostic Assessment ("Soil Type Assessment") → Fixable as lead magnet only, Fatal as standalone paid product
- Lie Eliminator Workbook → Fixable; highest-priority product candidate; needs buyer-language reframe + video/audio companion for somatic steps
- Online Course ("The Stuck Christian") → Fixable but Stage 3; build audience first
- Identity Installer (scoped 30-day program) → Fixable; highest IP differentiation; "My Evidence" mechanism is the hook; scope to 7 core identities, test with real users first
- Book ("What Soil Are You?") → Fixable long-term platform play; Fatal as near-term revenue vehicle
- Church Discipleship Assessment Tool → Fatal at current stage

**Services evaluated:**
- 1:1 Coaching (12-week engagement) → Solid; primary validation vehicle NOW; price $1,200–$1,800
- Church Workshop → Coaching Funnel → Fixable; right acquisition channel; requires one trusted pastor relationship to start
- Group Coaching Cohort → Fixable; Stage 2 after 10 validated 1:1 clients
- Intensive Format (1-day) → Fixable; Stage 3 premium tier after facilitation skill is proven
- Marriage Prep Coaching → Continue SC-00 engagement; don't build a brand around it yet
- Done-For-You Diagnostic Report → Fatal; wrong price point, belongs inside 12-week offer

## Next Steps
- Define the specific, nameable outcome of the 12-week coaching engagement (not "transformation" — a concrete sentence a buyer can say back)
- Identify one acquisition channel to master first (church workshop funnel recommended)
- Establish scope boundary: where coaching ends and referral to a therapist begins — written, not assumed
- Get 5 paying clients through the 12-week model before building any product
- Decide: Lie Eliminator workbook or Identity Installer (scoped) as first product build — not both simultaneously

## Key Decisions & Notes
- **Validation sequence:** validate → build → sell. Not build → sell → discover validation failed.
- The cheapest proof-of-concept is 5 people paying for a 4-session intensive built on Lie Eliminator + Identity Installer ($300–$500 each). Do this before building any product.
- Somatic component will face resistance from theologically conservative audiences — framing must lead with scripture, not methodology.
- The Identity Installer's "My Evidence" section (personal theological autobiography) is the product differentiator. No one else does this.
- The Lie Eliminator's 4-layer stronghold anatomy (belief + evidence + emotion + behavior) is the clinical differentiator. Most Christian lie work only addresses belief.
- Church workshop label: avoid "coaching" in conservative contexts — "Kingdom formation" or "soil work" language reduces resistance.
- Products ranked by current viability: (1) Lie Eliminator workbook, (2) Identity Installer scoped program, (3) Soil Type diagnostic as lead magnet, (4) Online course, (5) Book.
- Services ranked: (1) 1:1 12-week, (2) Church workshop funnel, (3) Group cohort, (4) Intensive, (5) Marriage prep (ongoing only).
- Three unanswered questions that determine whether any of this works: (1) specific nameable outcome, (2) one acquisition channel mastered, (3) explicit scope boundary vs. therapy.
