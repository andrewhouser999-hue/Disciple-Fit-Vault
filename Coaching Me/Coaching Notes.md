# Coaching Notes

***We become what we believe***

**God's will for your life:** *Is not a career path but a posture of your heart*
1 Thess 5:16-18 16 Rejoice always, pray without ceasing, give thanks in all circumstances; for this is the will of God in Christ Jesus for you.
Micah 6:8 He has told you, O man, what is good; and what does the Lord require of you  
but to do justice, and to love kindness, and to walk humbly with your God?

Know my most inward parts - My subconscious beliefs.
Ps 139:23-24 Search me, O God, and know my heart: try me, and know my thoughts: And see if there be any wicked way in me, and lead me in the way everlasting.

Connecting with Vulnerability, Impartiality, Empathy, & Wonder makes for a most powerful life changing time with clients. 

When working with someone who is stuck, work with the underlying victim anger first followed by developing the victor anger and then work with the forward movement. 

Inner work and ambition must be worked simultaneously

Those who are into self discovery are ambitious, but those who are ambitious are not necessarily dedicated to self discovery. Those who are both say, I am going to see the hard thing and go into the hard thing.

A genuine complement destroys an ego more than anything.  The ego is any way a person sees themselves. The hungry ghost longs for the complement while unable to handle it. 

Affirmation work - affirming self and others. Share something positive about someone and have others give the evidence of it.  It demolishes the ego that has defined the self perception. As the ego changes the perception of self and the world changes.

Appreciation and autonomy promote the greatest productivity and over that of money as motivation.
