# Covenant Identity — Arc Location Diagnostic — Personal
*Self-assessment for locating my current position on the covenant identity arc*

*System version: [[Covenant Identity — Arc Location Diagnostic]]*

---

## How to Use This

This document locates me on the covenant identity arc at a given moment. It is not a one-time assessment — it is meant to be returned to as the arc progresses. The results are most accurate when I complete Part 1 while something is emotionally live, not in a calm, reflective state. The arc moves below the surface; the questions here are designed to surface what is actually operating, not what I would like to be true.

The arc runs:

**Stage 1** (Affections mapped) → **Stage 2** (False identity named with emotional contact) → **Stage 3** (Covenant truth engaged) → **Stage 4a** (Cognitive distortion blocking) or **Stage 4b** (Implicit memory barrier) → **Telos** (Interior life qualities becoming operative)

Most people are not located cleanly in a single stage. Part 1 finds the edge of my current work. Part 2 profiles where the telos is emerging or absent. Both together produce the arc location in Part 3.

---

## Part 1 — Stage Location

Complete each check in sequence. Stop at the first check where the answer reveals an incomplete stage — that is my current working edge.

---

### Stage 1 Check — Affections Mapped

**Question:** In the area of life where I feel the most internal pressure right now, what am I actually trusting for security, significance, or identity — not what I believe I *should* trust, but what I am functionally depending on?

> *(Name it as specifically as possible — not "approval" but "I need [specific person/outcome] to respond a certain way before I feel okay." Not "success" but "I need [specific thing] to feel like I am not a failure.")*
>
>

**What is that dependency providing — security, significance, or identity? What would its loss cost me?**

> *(The cost is the tell. Name what would be true about me if this dependency were removed.)*
>
>

**Diagnostic check — all three must be present to proceed:**

- [ ] I can name the specific dependency — not a general category, but the particular thing I am trusting right now
- [ ] I can name what it is providing (security / significance / identity)
- [ ] I can name what its loss would cost me — and feel the weight of that

**If any box is unchecked:** I am in Stage 1. See [[Covenant Identity — Affections Discovery — In-Session Protocol]].

**If all three are checked:** Proceed to Stage 2 Check.

### Stage 2 Check — False Identity Named

**Question:** What does the affections picture reveal about who I believe I am? Name the identity-level lie — not a behavior to change, but the "I am only _____ when _____" or "Without _____, I am _____" statement the dependency is organized around.

> *(Write the lie in its own voice — the way it sounds inside, not a cleaned-up version.)*
>
>

**When I name it, what happens?**

> *(Describe the actual response — what I notice in my body, my emotional state, my instinct to argue with or agree with the statement.)*
>
>

**Is there weariness, grief, or fatigue directed at the lie itself — not at myself, but at how long this pattern has been running?**

> *(Not guilt. Not self-condemnation. Something closer to: "I am tired of this still being true.")*
>
>

**Diagnostic check:**

- [ ] The lie is named at the identity level ("I am only..." / "Without..., I am..."), not at the behavioral level ("I tend to...")
- [ ] Naming it produces emotional contact — a felt response in the body, not only intellectual recognition
- [ ] Something of weariness or grief is present toward the structure itself — the lie is not just identified, it is felt as failing

**If boxes 1–2 are unchecked:** Stage 2 incomplete — stay in Stage 1 work.

**If boxes 1–2 checked but box 3 unchecked:** Stage 2 partially complete — do not introduce covenant truth yet.

**If all three are checked:** Stage 2 complete. Proceed to Stage 3 Check.

### Stage 3 Check — Covenant Truth Engagement

Speak covenant truth directly against the named lie — the counter-statement the lie most needs to hear.

**What happens when I hold the covenant truth?**

> *(Describe the actual response — not what should happen.)*
>
>

**Locate my response:**

- [ ] **A — Felt movement:** The truth lands with some resonance — a shift, a warmth, an exhale, even partial. Something moves.

- [ ] **B — Active resistance:** Pushback arrives — I find myself explaining why it doesn't quite apply to my situation, articulating a counter-belief, cycling back through reasoning.

- [ ] **C — Cognitive receipt, felt gap:** I receive it as correct but it produces no felt movement. Knowing and feeling are disconnected.

**If A:** Stage 3 active — stay and deepen. Proceed to Part 2.

**If B:** Move to Stage 4a Check.

**If C:** Move to Stage 4b Check.

## Part 2 — Telos Profile

Rate each quality: **Present** (operative in actual experience), **Emerging** (showing in some contexts, not yet stable), or **Largely Absent** (rarely or never operative at a felt level).

| Interior Quality | What it looks like from the inside | Rating |
|---|---|---|
| **Settled** | Groundedness that does not depend on circumstances resolving — not moved from the root even when things are unresolved | **Largely Absent** |
| **Undivided** | Fully present in this moment, with this person — not split between what is and what might happen | **Emerging** |
| **Free** | Interior not owned by what others think, not hostage to outcomes, not shrunk by fear of what might be said | **Largely Absent** |
| **Moved Without Being Swept** | Fully inhabited emotional life that stays anchored — feel deeply without losing access to myself | **Emerging** |
| **Unhurried** | No background anxiety about what is not being accomplished; the person in front of me is the thing | **Largely Absent** |
| **Loved and Knowing It** | Not believing it — *knowing* it, operating from it as a lived fact | **Emerging** |
| **Truthful Without Cruelty** | Seeing and speaking clearly — love and truth arrive at the same time | **Insufficient data — assess directly** |
| **Purposeful Without Striving** | Knowing what I am for, expressing it — not doing the work and simultaneously proving I should be doing it | **Largely Absent** |
| **Available** | Interior facing outward before the encounter — stable enough to receive another's state without flinching | **Emerging** |

**What do I observe in the telos profile?**

> The four qualities rated Largely Absent — Settled, Free, Unhurried, Purposeful Without Striving — all share the same root: they require operating from received identity rather than proven identity. Each of them is blocked by the same structure the Pending Verdict lie maintains. When the verdict is open, the interior cannot settle, cannot be free from outcomes, cannot be unhurried, cannot move without simultaneously proving.
>
> The qualities rated Emerging — Undivided, Moved Without Being Swept, Loved and Knowing It, Available — are relational and emotional capacities that don't depend as directly on the verdict being closed. They have room to develop even while the arc work continues.
>
> Loved and Knowing It is the pivot quality. It is the one that, when it moves from Emerging to Present, would unlock the four Largely Absent qualities above it. The arc work on the Pending Verdict lie is not peripheral — it is aimed at the root of four simultaneous telos absences.
